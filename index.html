A brief introduction to E-mail

What exactly is E-mail?

E-mail is the exchange of computer stored messages through telecommunication.It is also the method of exchanging messages between people using electronic devices.E-mail is very popular for it's speedy transmission of information's and cost effectiveness.Hence information provided by a sender is delivered to a point where it can be retrieved and processed later on by one or more recipients of the sender's choice. In an E-mail system, there is a number and the sender sends the messages to the PC terminal of the recipient at a very high speed.There is no need of physical presence of both the sender and the receiver.The sender can enter the messsages and receiver and retrive it whenever he/she is free.The message will remain in PC's memory.The transmission of messages is usually done through landlines (telephones) or satellite communications or through margin cables. E-mail is most commonly used features of the internet.The SMTP(Simple main transfer protocol) is used to send E-mail and POP3(Post office protocol 3) is used to receive E-mail.



E-mail Address

Each user of email is assigned a unique name for his email account. This name is known as E-mail address. Different users can send and receive messages according to the e-mail address.


E-mail is generally of the form username@domainname. For example, webmaster@tutorialspoint.com is an e-mail address where webmaster is username and tutorialspoint.com is domain name.

  • 1.The username and the domain name are separated by @ (at) symbol.
  • 2.E-mail addresses are not case sensitive.
    • 3.Spaces are not allowed in e-mail address.
